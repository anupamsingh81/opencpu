Risk Model Information :

This risk model estimates the risk of Death and Rebleed from patients presenting features at admission. Death and Rebleed within a duration of 30 days were assessed in this prognostic cohort. The model was developed based on 300 consecutive Upper GI Bleed patients in gastroenterology unit of a tertiary care hospital . 70% of patients were randomly assigned to a derivation cohort and 30% to the validation cohort.
